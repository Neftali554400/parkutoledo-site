Contractor & Vendor Parking

Contractors and vendors working on campus must register all vehicles through the contractor portal.

Vehicle Registration Required

All contractors, vendors, and third-party service providers working on the University of Toledo campus must register their vehicles through the ParkUToledo contractor portal before parking on university property.

Unregistered vehicles in contractor areas are subject to citation and towing at the owner's expense. Please ensure all vehicles in your crew are registered prior to beginning any campus work.

Register Your Vehicle

Use the contractor portal to register all vehicles before arriving on campus.

Contractor Vehicle Portal

Key Requirements

  • → All vehicles must be registered in the contractor portal before parking on campus.
  • → Contractors must park in designated contractor/service vehicle areas only.
  • → A permit code provided by your university contact may be required — check with your project manager.
  • → Loading and unloading must occur in designated service areas. Do not block fire lanes or accessible spaces.
  • → Citations issued to unregistered contractor vehicles are the responsibility of the contracting company.

Questions? Contact the ParkUToledo office at info@parkutoledo.com before your first day on campus.
