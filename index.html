Do I need a permit on weekends?

Yes. Enforcement is active Saturday–Sunday 9AM–5PM in all designated areas.

How do I purchase a permit?

Log in to the Parking Portal to purchase daily, monthly, or semester permits.

How do I appeal a citation?

File an appeal within 10 calendar days via the Parking Portal. Decisions are final.
