Game Day Parking — Permit Holder Info

Important information for D, C, and K permit holders on football and basketball game days at the University of Toledo.

Permit Holders: Certain parking areas are closed to regular permit holders on game days. Review the closure table below and plan accordingly. Questions? Email athleticticketoffice@utoledo.edu.

Football Game Days

On home football game days, several parking areas adjacent to the Glass Bowl are reserved for game-day parking and are closed to regular permit holders beginning several hours before kickoff. Closures are typically lifted 1 hour after the game ends.

Permit Holder Instructions — D, C, K Permits

  • → D permit holders: Alternative parking available in Lot 6 and Lot 7 during game-day closures.
  • → C permit holders: Park in any open C or D zone not listed in the closure table below.
  • → K permit holders: Contact the Athletic Ticket Office for game-day reserved parking options.
  • → All vehicles must vacate closed areas by the closure time listed or risk citation and towing.

Area Closures — Football

Area / Lot Closes For Permit Holders Reopens
Lot 1 (Glass Bowl North) 4 hours before kickoff 1 hour after game
Lot 2 (Glass Bowl South) 4 hours before kickoff 1 hour after game
Lot 3 (Stadium East) 2 hours before kickoff 1 hour after game
Lot 4 (Overpass) 2 hours before kickoff 1 hour after game

Basketball Game Days

During home basketball games at Savage Arena, select parking areas near the arena are reserved for game-day use. Closures are typically shorter than football — approximately 2 hours before tip-off to 30 minutes after the game.

Area / Lot Closes For Permit Holders Reopens
Lot A (Savage Arena) 2 hours before tip-off 30 min after game
Lot B (Arena West) 2 hours before tip-off 30 min after game

Questions About Game Day Parking?

Contact the Athletic Ticket Office for game-specific parking questions and reserved options for K permit holders:
athleticticketoffice@utoledo.edu
