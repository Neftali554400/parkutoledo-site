Find Parking

With a campus size of more than 1,040 acres, where you park at The University of Toledo matters. Find parking at the Main Campus and Health Science Campus to best fit your needs by using the interactive map below.

Guests to campus may purchase day or month permits depending on the length of their visit. Guest can also purchase hourly parking via the ParkMobile app. After purchasing a guest permit, guests may park in any C Permit area on campus.

Retirees are eligible for an "A" Parking Permit once they are coded as a retiree in the UToledo system. Retiree permits are the same price as the current "A Permit rate."

Looking for transportation services? Visit The University of Toledo Transportation Services.
