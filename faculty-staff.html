Faculty & Staff Parking

Annual and monthly permit options for University of Toledo employees, including payroll deduction.

Employee Parking Permits

University of Toledo faculty and staff may purchase annual or monthly parking permits through the ParkUToledo portal. Permits are zone-specific and must be displayed in your vehicle at all times during enforcement hours.

Employees may take advantage of payroll deduction to spread permit costs across pay periods. Contact the Parking Office for more information on setting up payroll deduction.

Payroll Deduction Available: Eligible employees can pay for annual permits through payroll deduction. Contact info@parkutoledo.com to enroll.

Key Rules for Faculty & Staff

  • • Permits must be displayed or registered to your license plate at all times during enforcement hours.
  • • Faculty/staff permits are valid only in designated employee zones.
  • • Enforcement hours: Mon–Fri 7AM–10PM, Sat–Sun 9AM–5PM.
  • • Permits are non-transferable between employees.
  • • Report lost or stolen permits to the Parking Office immediately.

Frequently Asked Questions

Contact the ParkUToledo office at info@parkutoledo.com to enroll in payroll deduction. You will need to provide your employee ID and the permit type you wish to purchase.
Faculty/staff permit holders are not authorized to park in visitor or student zones during enforcement hours. Violations will result in a citation. After enforcement hours, most areas are open to all valid permit holders.
Contact the Parking Office in advance. Temporary zone accommodations may be available for specific situations such as medical needs, event setup, or departmental activities. Email info@parkutoledo.com with your request.
Log in to the Parking Portal and navigate to Citations. Appeals must be submitted within 10 calendar days of the citation date. See the Citations page for full details.
