Daily & Hourly Parking

Short-term parking is available via the ParkMobile app or daily permit purchase through the portal.

ParkMobile — Hourly Parking

ParkMobile is the fastest way to pay for short-term parking on campus. Download the app, find your zone number on the meter or signage, and start your session in seconds.

  • ✓ No coins, no meter — pay from your phone
  • ✓ Extend your session remotely
  • ✓ Get low-time alerts before your session expires
  • ✓ Available in Zones 11, 12, 13, and 18
