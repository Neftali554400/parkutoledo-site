Welcome to ParkUToledo’s parking portal.

Please see instructions below and contact us at info@parkutoledo.com with any questions.

ParkUToledo Permit Registration

  1. Create an Account or Login
    1. UToledo Students, Employees & Affiliates - Login with your UToledo Credentials
    2. Visitors - Create an Account
    3. Returning Visitor - Login to your Parking Portal
  2. Obtain a Parking Permit
  3. Park on UToledo’s Main & Health Science Campuses
    1. Please note, parking in metered spaces/areas requires separate payment.
    2. All vehicles on campus must follow ParkUToledo’s Rules & Regulations
    3. Please remember all vehicles on campus must be registered with a permit purchased online in advance or by utilizing the ParkMobile App or a parking meter when visiting campus.
    4. Parking permits are enforced between semesters, during academic breaks and on weekends.
      1. Permit Enforcement Hours:
        • Monday through Friday 7AM-10PM
        • Saturday & Sunday 9AM-5PM
    5. On weekdays from 5PM-7AM and on Saturdays & Sundays, active permits may park in areas designated for other permits. Exceptions include: reserved, disabled, metered, patient and no overnight parking spaces/areas – which are enforced 24/7.
    6. For helpful parking information please visit: ParkUToledo’s Important Parking Info.
