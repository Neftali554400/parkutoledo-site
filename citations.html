Parking Citations

Pay your citation, understand enforcement hours, or file an appeal through the ParkUToledo portal.

Parking Enforcement Hours

Monday – Friday: 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM
Saturday – Sunday: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
A valid parking permit or daily pass is required in all designated areas during these hours.

Pay or Appeal a Citation

You will be directed to the ParkUToledo Parking Portal.

How the Citation Process Works

1

Receive a Notice

A citation is placed on your vehicle or sent electronically to the registered vehicle owner. The notice includes the citation number, violation, amount due, and the deadline to pay or appeal.

2

Log In to the Portal

Visit vpermit.com/parkutoledo and log in with your UTAD credentials (students/staff) or citation number (visitors). Navigate to the Citations section to view your violation details.

3

Pay or Submit an Appeal

Pay your citation securely online, or submit an appeal if you believe the citation was issued in error. Appeals must be filed within 10 calendar days of the citation date.

Citation Appeals

Log in to the ParkUToledo Parking Portal at vpermit.com/parkutoledo. Navigate to "My Citations," select the citation you wish to appeal, and click "File Appeal." Provide your reason for appealing and any supporting documentation. Appeals must be submitted within 10 calendar days of the citation date.

Yes. All appeal decisions made by the ParkUToledo appeals committee are final. If your appeal is denied, the citation amount must be paid in full. Payment arrangements may be available — contact info@parkutoledo.com for more information.
Unpaid citations may be sent to collections. For students, unpaid fines may be placed on your university account, which can affect registration, transcript release, and graduation. Vehicles with 3 or more unpaid citations are subject to immobilization (booting) or towing.

Immobilization (Booting)

Vehicles with 3 or more unpaid citations are subject to immobilization. A boot will be placed on the vehicle, preventing it from being driven.

  • • To have a boot removed, all outstanding citations must be paid in full plus a boot removal fee.
  • • Vehicles left immobilized for an extended period may be towed at the owner's expense.
  • • Contact the Parking Office immediately if your vehicle has been booted: info@parkutoledo.com
