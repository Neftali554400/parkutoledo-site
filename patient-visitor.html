Patient & Patient Visitor Parking

Information and voluntary permit options for patients and their visitors parking at the University of Toledo.

Patient Visitor Parking Program

The ParkUToledo Patient Visitor Parking Program is a voluntary program that provides qualifying individuals with a special permit for medical-related visits to campus. Participation is voluntary and requires documentation each semester.

Green Parking Area — Enforced 24/7

The designated green parking area for patient visitors is enforced 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. A valid permit or patient visitor pass is required at all times, including evenings and weekends.

Program Requirements

  • → Participation is voluntary and open to qualifying patients and patient visitors.
  • → Medical documentation must be submitted each semester to remain enrolled.
  • → Permits are issued for the vehicle's license plate on file.
  • → Permits do not guarantee a specific space — they grant access to designated areas only.
  • → Questions: info@parkutoledo.com
