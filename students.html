Student Parking Permits

Full-time and part-time students enrolled at The University of Toledo may purchase parking permits through the ParkUToledo portal. Permits are required in all designated parking areas during enforcement hours.

Semester permits are the most economical option for students who park on campus regularly. Daily permits are also available for occasional use through the portal or ParkMobile app.

Pricing Note: Permit prices are subject to change each academic year. Visit the Parking Portal for current pricing.

Key Parking Rules for Students

  • • Parking permits are required Mon–Fri 7AM–10PM and Sat–Sun 9AM–5PM in all designated areas.
  • • Permits are non-transferable and must be displayed in the vehicle at all times.
  • • Student permits are only valid in student-designated zones.
  • • Citations may be appealed within 10 calendar days via the Parking Portal.
  • • Vehicles with 3 or more unpaid citations may be immobilized (booted).
