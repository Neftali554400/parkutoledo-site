Metropolis.io Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Introduction

Metropolis Technologies, Inc. and its subsidiaries collect, use, store, and share personal information through websites, mobile applications, physical facilities, and partner interactions. This policy covers all "Services" linking to or subject to this notice.

Key sections include privacy rights and choices, cookie technologies, and an Automated License Plate Reader (ALPR) supplemental notice (Exhibit A).

Information We Collect When You Interact With Our Services

The company collects:

  • Basic identifying information: name, email, phone, vehicle license plate, account credentials
  • Vehicle information: ALPR captures video/photos of vehicles and plates with date, time, location
  • Device identifiers: IP address, cookies, beacons, pixel tags, mobile ad identifiers
  • Biometric information: facial recognition data at offices and facilities
  • Network activity: browsing history, email interactions, service usage
  • Precise geolocation: device location when enabled
  • Financial information: processed through third-party payment processors
  • Commercial information: purchased services, preferences, usage patterns
  • User-generated content: surveys, reviews, testimonials
  • Audio/visual: photographs, videos of vehicles
  • Professional information: resumes, application materials, event-collected data

How We Collect Personal Information

Collection occurs through three methods:

  1. Directly from you: account creation, purchases, facility visits, surveys, communications
  2. Automatically via tracking: websites, services, emails, advertisements using cookies, pixels, beacons, SDKs
  3. From third parties: business customers, service providers, data analytics companies, payment processors

How We Use Personal Information

Metropolis uses collected data to:

  • Provide, improve, and operate Services
  • Communicate and provide customer service
  • Train AI systems, including large language models and machine learning
  • Market, advertise, and conduct promotions
  • Analyze usage and personalize experiences
  • Create de-identified or aggregated information
  • Prevent fraud and maintain security
  • Comply with legal obligations

How We Disclose Personal Information

Information is shared with:

  • Service providers performing services
  • Business customers (hotels, employers, facilities)
  • Corporate affiliates
  • Advertising partners using cookies and tracking for targeted ads

Disclosures also occur:

  • To comply with legal process and protect interests
  • In business transactions (mergers, acquisitions, financing)
  • Text message opt-in data is excluded from third-party sharing

Cross-Border Data Transfers

Personal information may be stored and processed internationally, including in the United States. Data protection standards vary by location. The company uses standard contractual clauses where applicable.

Retention of Personal Information

Data is retained as long as necessary for collection purposes, business needs, and legal requirements.

Links to Third-Party Sites

Services contain links to independent third-party websites. Metropolis is not responsible for their privacy policies.

Do Not Track

The company does not honor or respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals.

Your Privacy Rights and Choices

Depending on location and jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Know/access/portability: Request confirmation of processing and obtain copies
  • Delete: Request personal information deletion
  • Correct: Request information corrections
  • Opt out: Refuse targeted advertising, data sales, cross-context behavioral advertising, or profiling

To opt out of data sales: Click Here

To opt out via cookies: clear cookies, revisit site, select "Decline" in banner. This blocks marketing/analytics cookies while maintaining necessary functionality cookies.

Additional choices:

  • Unsubscribe from marketing emails via email footer links
  • Stop marketing texts by replying STOP
  • Adjust push notifications in mobile app settings
  • Revoke geolocation via device settings
  • Uninstall mobile apps anytime (device identifier persists)
  • Delete account on mobile devices

Metropolis verifies identity before processing requests and allows appeals where legally permitted.

Children

Services are not intended for users under 16 years old.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

Policy changes appear on this page with updated effective dates. Users should regularly check for updates.

Accessibility

Contact Metropolis to obtain printed or alternative format copies.

Exhibit A — Supplemental ALPR Privacy Notice

Authorized Purposes

ALPR Systems collect information for parking enforcement, rate validation, vehicle inventories, and purposes outlined in the main Privacy Policy.

ALPR Systems: searchable databases from mobile/fixed cameras with computer algorithms converting license plate images to readable data.

ALPR Information: data collected through ALPR Systems.

Authorized Personnel

Lot checkers, parking attendants, enforcement agents, facility managers, and contractors access ALPR Systems. Personnel receive information security and local facility training.

ALPR System Monitoring

Metropolis IT Information Security monitors collected information. In California, access records showing date, time, and queried data elements are maintained. Information is restricted to authorized purposes.

Disclosures of ALPR Information

Information is disclosed per the main Privacy Policy. Parking violation information goes to third-party collection agencies where permitted.

Responsible Personnel

Implementation overseen by Director of Operations Support and Director of Information Security.

Information Regarding ALPR Information Accuracy and Corrections

Metropolis reviews ALPR Information accuracy by comparing customer records and parking equipment data. A customer dispute procedure addresses inaccurate charges.

Retention of ALPR Information

Data is retained per the main Privacy Policy. Paid customer information: minimum 90 days. Unpaid customer information with issued citations: minimum 5 years.

Your Privacy Choices

Under California law, consumers may opt out of "sharing for cross-context behavioral advertising" and data "sales."

Opt-out procedure: Clear browser cookies, revisit the site, select "Decline" in the cookie banner. This blocks marketing/advertising and non-essential analytics cookies.

Right to opt-out of sale: The company has not sold personal information in the past 12 months. To request future non-sale, visit the opt-out form.

Biometric Information Privacy Notice

Last updated: June 10, 2025

Why Metropolis Is Providing This Biometric Information Privacy Notice

Some collected information may be considered biometric under applicable laws, requiring specific notice regarding collection and processing.

Why and How We Collect Biometric Information

Metropolis deploys cameras in offices and operated facilities. Video recordings generate facial geometry data to enable the use of facial recognition technology. This supports contactless parking, security, hospitality, and facility protection.

How We Use Biometric Information

Biometric information is used to provide and improve Services, enable contactless parking access, support security and facility operations, and for other purposes described in the main Privacy Policy.

Privacy Rights

Individuals may email privacy@metropolis.io to exercise rights:

  • Request to know: disclosure of biometric collection and third-party sharing
  • Request to opt out/withdraw consent: refuse collection, sharing, or sale
  • Request to delete: removal of biometric information
  • Request to access: obtain contact information for third parties receiving data

Rights are subject to exceptions. Identity verification may be required. If data no longer exists, Metropolis will notify the individual. Refusals may be appealed to privacy@metropolis.io.

Retention of Biometric Information

Biometric information is retained until: (a) initial collection purpose is satisfied or (b) three years after last interaction with Metropolis.
