Visitor & Guest Parking

Welcome to The University of Toledo. Parking is available via daily permit or the ParkMobile app.

How to Get a Daily Parking Permit

1

Visit the Parking Portal

Go to vpermit.com/parkutoledo. Guest users can purchase a daily permit without creating an account.

2

Select "Daily Visitor Permit"

Choose the date of your visit and the visitor parking zone. Enter your vehicle's license plate number to register your permit digitally — no printed permit is required.

3

Pay & Park

Complete your payment online. Park in the designated visitor area. Your license plate is your permit — enforcement officers verify digitally. Starting at $5 per day.

Hourly Parking

Park by the Hour with ParkMobile

Download the ParkMobile app for fast, contactless hourly parking. Available in metered areas on campus (Zones 11, 12, 13, 18).
